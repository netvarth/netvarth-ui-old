Q1) Which cities are Jaldee operational in?
A1) We are currently available in whole of India.
Q2) How will consumers get notified about any changes in check-in?
A2) Whenever you make any changes like cancel a check-in, add delay to the waitlist a push notification (app) or email (if given) will be sent to the consumer.
Q3) I provide service in multiple locations. How do I include and show them all?
A3) If your business comes under the following domains, you can add multiple locations.
  • Healthcare
  • Professional Consulting
  • Religious/Priests
  • Vastu/Astrologer
  • Veterinary/Pet Care
The working hours can be the same for both locations.
Q4) How do I register my business on jaldee.com?
A4) Log on to www.jaldee.com or download and install Jaldee Provider App from Google Play Store/Apple store. Click on “Service Provider / Business [Go]“ and then click “Become a Jaldee Partner [Sign Up]“. You will be redirected to the signup page. Fill the required fields and proceed with setting up your profile in Jaldee as per the indications given.
Q5) I never asked to be listed but I am still listed on jaldee.com without any prior notification. Why?
A5) We at jaldee.com have data teams that search for and find out the top service providers in any locality and gather information about them so that they can be listed on our website. In all cases we call or visit the service provider and verify the details thus collected. In your particular case we would have spoken to a staff member at your organization. In case you don’t want to be listed please mail us on support@jaldee.com or call us on 8880125000 and we will have one of our senior team members speak to you and explain. If you still want to go ahead and delist please let the team members know when they speak to you and we will delist you immediately.
Q6) How can I deactivate jaldee.com account?
A6) Please mail us on support@jaldee.com or call us on 8880125000.
Q7) How are the service providers rated?
A7) After each service is completed, our consumers are asked to rate the service providers on different parameters and judge the overall satisfaction of the service by giving stars
Q8) How can I add my bank account details?
A8) It is an easy process, after signing in just go to the Payment settings on the settings page then add email id to your account from “My account“, if not already added and select CC/ DC/ Net Banking/UPI or Paytm and enter your account details. Once this is done our support team will verify your account and then you can accept payments from your consumers.
Q9) Can I give discounts to my consumers?
A9) Yes, you can give discount to your consumers, create discounts in Settings>Bill discounts which can be applied on consumer bill.
Q10) How QManager can help me in my business?
A10) QManager helps you to manage the working hours and ensure seamless workflow from the point of check-in to the waitlist to the actual appointment. Since we provide the estimated waiting time consumers can reach for the service at your premises on time which avoids waiting in long queues. Also, locations and services can be managed from here.
Q11) What you mean by Same day online check-in?
A11) Online check-in allows you full control over online consumer check-in. If this button is enabled, check-in is open for consumers and if this button is disabled, consumers can only view your profile at their dashboard,but cannot check-in online for any service offered.
Q12) How is waiting time calculated?
A12) Waiting time refers to the time consumer waits to avail his/her service offered at your premises. There are four ways through which waiting time is calculated.
  • Automatic (Machine Learning)
  • Manual
  • Token System
  • No Token

Q13) Can I accept online check-in for a future date?
A13) Yes, you can accept check-in for future by using our advanced feature “ Future check-in “ . If you check Future button under QManager settings, your consumer will have an option to check-in for future dates.
Q14) What is Token System?
A14) If you check “Token System“ button your consumer will get a token number when he checks-in for a service. This token number is displayed in the consumer dashboard with which consumers can have a brief idea about his position in the waitlist for a particular queue.
Q15) What is base location?
A15) Location details you have saved at the time of profile activation process is set by default as the base location of your business. You can also change your base location by editing the same and choose another location through “Google Maps“ .
Q16) Can I add multiple locations for my business?
A16) Yes you can add multiple locations through Add New Location from QManager, only if your business or service provided comes under the following domains:
  • Healthcare
  • Professional Consulting
  • Religious/Astrologer
  • Vastu/Astrologer
  • Veterinary/Pet Care

Q17) What is a Service?
A17) It is the service you or your business provide. When you create a service corresponding to the service you provide you have to give the name of the service, estimated duration, price etc. And the added service should be added to the working hours. When consumers search for you in Jaldee the services created by you will be listed under your profile.
Q18) How can I delete a service?
A18) You cannot delete a service. Instead you can disable it. The disabled service will not be listed in your public profile.
Q19) What happens if there are any objectionable contents in my profile?
A19) If any such content is found, Jaldee has the right to disable your account.
Q20) Can I avail prepayments from my consumers?
A20) Yes, you can receive prepayment by enabling the prepayment toggle button while creating a service. You can also set the amount to be collected as prepayment.
Q21) Can I set tax for my services?
A21) Yes, you can set tax for your services by enabling the tax applicable toggle button if you have already set the tax to be collected in the Billing&PoS>Tax Settings
Q22) How can I communicate with my consumers?
A22) You can communicate with the consumers in different ways, via “Send Message“ option in check-ins tab or by sending push notifications via mobile app/email/ for the actions like delay in wait time, service completion etc.
Q23) How to set the number of consumers served at the same time?
A23) While creating or editing a Working hour you can set the number of consumers that can be served at a time and the waiting time will automatically get adjusted.
Q24) How to limit the maximum number of consumers to be served for a day?
A24) While creating or editing a Working hour you can set the maximum number of consumers to be served for a day. For example if we set 10 as the maximum capacity, only 10 consumers will be served at the same time, if 2 of the consumers from the 10 consumers who have checked-in has started availing the service, 2 new check-ins will be accepted.
Q25) How to update my business profile details?
A25) You can update your business profile in Settings>Profile & Search.
Q26) Created a business account with Jaldee, but not listed in Jaldee public search?
A26) Enable public search in Settings>Profile & Search and get listed in Jaldee public search.
Q27) How secure is jaldee.com?
A27) Jaldee uses the most reliable Amazon Web Services (AWS) to store the data, so that your data will be safe and secure.
Q28) What is Jaldee Verification?
A28) After creating a business account, you will be eligible to get verified with jaldee.com. Jaldee has 3 levels of verifications.
  • Basic
  • Basic Plus
  • Advanced
All three levels have their own verification criteria when you fulfill those you will get verified by Jaldee. A Jaldee verified flag(golden crown, copper crown, charcoal crown) will be shown with your profile in public search results.
Q29) Are there any additional charges for Jaldee Verification?
A29) There are three levels of Jaldee verification Basic, Basic Plus and Advanced . Basic is free of cost, but charges are applicable for Basic plus and Advanced level verification.
Q30) What do you mean by Jaldee Search Keywords?
A30) Jaldee Search Keywords used to help your business to list on top of Jaldee search results. You can create suitable keywords related to your business. If you are Dentist and you have specialized in Paediatric Dentistry you can create keyword naming “Paediatric“. So when a consumer search for a pediatric dentist, higher is the chance for your profile to be listed in the top of search results.
Q31) What is Basic Information in business profile?
A31) General and basic information about your business you have given at the time of profile activation process is displayed here.You have the permission to edit each section under Basic Information.
Q32) What are specializations?
A32) In specialization you can add what you are specialized in. For example, if you are a lawyer you can add Criminal Lawyer, Civil Lawyer, Family Lawyer etc as your specializations.
Q33) Can I add images for my business?
A33) Yes you can add images about your business. Images and photos grab attention and make your profile more engaging. Using images in your profile will result in increased consumer attention and can give a boost in consumer check-ins. Photo Gallery provides view options and add multiple images option.
Q34) Where can I add my tax settings?
A34) You can add your GST number and tax percentage under Settings>Billing/PoS>Tax settings. These settings will be reflected in consumer bill, if tax is enabled for services or items.
Q35) How do I adjust my payment settings?
A35) Making it as easy as possible for your consumers to pay is essential for growing your business. Through Payment Settings you can input your Paytm/CC/DC/Net Banking/UPI information on Jaldee. You can select either choose “Paytm“ or “CC/DC/Net Banking/UPI“ or both. For enabling Paytm payments you need to enter your mobile number and submit. For CC/DC/Net Banking/UPI fill out all the fields displayed there and save. Jaldee bank is also an option for providers.
Q36) What is Jaldee Bank option under Payment Settings?
A36) Jaldee Bank option when selected, a customer can pay online for the services directly to the Jaldee Bank and in turn, the Jaldee team will reimburse the amount to the service provider. Jaldee bank is available for all providers.
Q37) What are the payment methods that I can accept from my consumers?
A37) For prepayments your consumers can use Paytm, Credit card, Debit card, Net Banking and UPI. You can also accept cash payment from consumers for direct payments.
Q38) How can I know that my Payment Settings are verified?
A38) To verify the payment settings please contact our support team who will verify your settings and let you know about it. After verification you can also see your settings verified in Payment Settings page(✔ symbol). Payment verification is necessary to receive online payments from your consumers.
