About Us

Our Products & Services

www.jaldee.com is a web portal connecting service providers with customers. Jaldee is an all India platform listing thousands of doctors/professionals/technicians and all service areas including healthcare, homecare, personal care and legal/financial care. The motto of Jaldee is seamless connectivity of service providers/business enterprises with potential customers. Elimination of queues, wiping out unproductive & boring waiting times, is the motivation & aim of Jaldee.

Two-way connection of doctors & patients/service providers & customers, and hassle-free appointments are the highlights of Jaldee. Doctors get unlimited patient check-ins, cost-free & labour-free, and patients get competitive & within-budget services of doctors & specialists nearby & across India, avoiding queues.

Services of professionals including doctors, lawyers, architects, beauticians, tax/finance experts, chartered accountants and scores of other technical/ service area experts are listed with Jaldee.com; making it an exhaustive hub of all services. As the word meaning in Hindi suggests, Jaldee (orJaldi) enables fast or quick access to any service.

Uninterrupted and quick access to everyday needs effectively prevents stress situations, and releases lots of extra time & energy for vital areas. Cutting short the waiting time & boredom, jaldee.com brings in ease of access across all service areas. We mean to add happiness in each service.

At jaldee.com, doctors and other professionals are listed after verification of legal permissions and background research. The specialty doctors of Jaldee include Dentists, Physiotherapists, Gynaecologists, Paediatricians, Psychologists, ENT specialists, etc.

Jaldee has listed thousands of institutions and business houses like Hospitals, Beauty clinics, Salon, Spa/Yoga, Diagnostic labs, Vastu/Astrology, Banks/NBFCs, Restaurants etc. across India. The biggest advantage of listing with Jaldee.com is the endless flow of customers /clients giving a tremendous boost to customer access, business prosperity, online presence, and fame.

Being a Jaldee partner, corporates & professionals can realize brand creation & brand building without extra cost on advertising. Customers access the full details & profile of the provider along with GPS location. They can book an appointment ‘on the go’ through jaldee, and get in virtual queue. Mobile alerts on the wait-time-status help the customers/patients avoid waiting in real queue, and be on time for the appointment.

Utilities like Jaldee Pay, Jaldee Coupon, online appointment and online queue management improve ease of business & customer reach for the service provider/business. Routine operations like appointment scheduling, queue management, billing and cash receipts are taken care of by Jaldee, effectively bridging the gap between provider & consumer.

Jaldee Soft Pvt. Ltd. India, that owns Jaldee, works in partnership with Jaldee Inc. USA. The cloud based platform of Jaldee.com is secure and Smartphone friendly. The Jaldee app is available in Android and iOS versions.
