Jaldee Products

Jaldee QManager

Jaldee QManager helps your consumers to take online tokens and appointments in advance. The consumers need to reach your premises only a few minutes before their turn. In this way consumers don't need to wait around long in crowded queues and create unwanted chaos in your premises.

Consumers get mobile alerts to know when their turn is coming up. Within app communication between providers and consumers. Display board for displaying the status of consumers in your premises JaldeePay enables you to receive online payments. You can avoid cash/cards transactions.

Jaldee tracks consumers real-time location. Get the estimated time of arrival and plan accordingly.

Jaldee Online

Create your online profile and be visible to consumers across the country. Consumers can find you online and avail your services.

Get listed in Jaldee and increase your brand awareness Consumers can search you using keywords such as specialisations, domains, services etc Display your amenities by uploading images to the image gallery GPS and google map integration help your consumers to locate you easily.

Get online enquiries from consumers through Jaldee and give more awareness about your services. Enable Jaldee Coupons and attract more consumers.

Jaldee PoS/Billing

Affordable cloud based PoS. No separate hardware required. Data is safe in the cloud servers. Cashless transactions. Jaldee is fully integrated with Credit card, Debit card and UPI Consumer one-click self pay system.

Reduce no show up by accepting prepayments. Supports electronic receipts.
