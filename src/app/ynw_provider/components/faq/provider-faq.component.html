Q1) HOW TO UPDATE MY BUSINESS PROFILE DETAILS?
A1) You can update your business profile in Settings>Profile.

Q2) CREATED BUSINESS ACCOUNT WITH JALDEE BUT NOT LISTED IN JALDEE PUBLIC SEARCH?
A2) Enable public search in Settings>Profile and get listed in jaldee public search.

Q3) WHAT IS JALDEE VERIFICATION?
A3) After creating a business account, you will be eligible to get verified with Jaldee.com. Jaldee has 3 levels of verifications. ie Basic, Basic Plus, Premium. All three levels have their own verification criteria when you fulfill those you will get verified by jaldee. A jaldee verified flag will be shown with your profile in public search results.

Q4) ARE THERE ANY ADDITIONAL CHARGES FOR JALDEE VERIFICATION?
A4) There are three levels of jaldee verification Basic, Basic Plus and Premium. Basic is free of cost but charges are applicable for Basic Plus & Premium level verification.

Q5) WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY ADWORDS?
A5) Adwords are the keywords used to help your business to list on top of jaldee search results. You can create suitable adwords related with your business. If you are Dentist and you have specialized in Pediatric Dentistry you can create adword naming “Pediatric”. So when a consumer search for a pediatric dentist, higher is the chance for your profile to be listed in the top of search results.

Q6) WHAT IS BASIC INFORMATION IN BUSINESS PROFILE?
A6) General and basic information about your business you have given at the time of profile activation process is displayed here.You have the permission to edit each section under Basic Information.

Q7) WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY LOCATION AMENITIES?
A7) Location amenities are resources, conveniences, facilities or benefits continuously offered to the consumers for their use and/or enjoyment, with or without charges at your premises. Amenities are regarded as increasingly important to the growth of your business. It affects customer decision in in choosing certain types of firm (for example Parking type, Available 24 hrs etc).

Q8) WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY PRIVACY SETTINGS?
A8) Controlling your privacy settings (Phone number & Email address) is an important part of protecting your information. Here the system allows you to add, edit or delete both phone number and email address. You can also select who can see your information online.

Q9) WHAT ARE SPECIALIZATIONS?
A9) In specialization you can add what you are specialized in. For example, if you are a lawyer you can add Criminal Lawyer, Civil Lawyer, Family Lawyer etc as your specializations.

Q10) WHAT DO YOU MEANT BY LANGUAGES KNOWN?
A10) Adding different languages widens the door to attract more consumers.You can select the languages you wish to communicate with the consumers.

Q11) WHAT IS ADDITIONAL INFO?
A11) In additional information you can give any additional information pertaining to your business fields, such as Educational qualification, Awards & Recognitions, Reference URL etc.

Q12) CAN I ADD IMAGES FOR MY BUSINESS?
A12) Yes you can add images about your business. Images and photos grab attention and make your profile more engaging. Using images in your profile will result in increased consumer attention and can give a boost in consumer check-ins. Photo Gallery provides view options and add multiple images option.

Q13) EXPLAIN WORKING HOURS?
A13) Your business’ working hours can be set under Settings>Queues. This working hours will be visible to the consumer in your public search profile. You can also disable or edit the same.

Q14) WHAT IS YOUR SOCIAL MEDIA?
A14) These days social media is regarded as the face of any business. You can add links to your social media profiles. Social media link to services like Twitter and Facebook allows visitors to access your social media profiles and get more information about your business.

Q1) HOW CHECK-IN MANAGER CAN HELP ME IN MY BUSINESS?
A1) Waitlist manager helps you to manage the queue and ensure seamless workflow from the point of check-in to the wait list to the actual appointment. Customers can book your service from their mobile/computer from anywhere anytime. Since we provide the estimated waiting time customers can reach for the service at your premises on time which avoids overcrowding and unnecessary waiting. Using jaldee digital payments customer can pay you online or by cash. Jaldee offers different attractive coupons which will attract more customers to you.

Q2) WHAT You MEAN BY ACCEPT ONLINE CHECK-IN?
A2) Accept online Check-in allows you full control over online consumer check-in. If this button is enabled, check-in is open for consumers and if this button is disabled, consumers can only view your profile at their dashboard,but cannot check-in online for any service offered.

Q3) HOW IS WAITING TIME CALCULATED?
A3) Waiting time refers to the time consumer waits to avail his/her service offered at your premise. There are two ways through which waiting time is calculated. The first one is Automatic Machine Learning and the second one is Fixed.

Q4) CAN I ACCEPT ONLINE CHECK-IN FOR THE FUTURE?
A4) Of course you can accept Check-in for future by using our advanced feature “Accept Future Check-in”. If you enable Accept Future Check-in your customer will have an option to do Check-in for future dates.

Q5) WHAT IS SHOW TOKEN?
A5) If you enable “Show token” your consumer will get a token number when he checks-in for a service. This token number is displayed in the consumer dashboard with which consumer can have a brief idea about his position in the waitlist for a particular queue. If you disable this button your consumers will get only the estimated wait time.

Q6) WHAT IS BASE LOCATION?
A6) Location details you have saved at the time of profile activation process is set by default as the base location of your business. You can also change your base location by editing the same and choose another location through “Google Maps”.

Q7) CAN I ADD MULTIPLE LOCATIONS FOR MY BUSINESS?
A7) Yes you can add multiple locations through Add New Location on Wait list Manager, but the time schedule should not overlap.

Q8) WHAT IS A SERVICE?
A8) It is the service you or your business provide. When you create a service corresponding to the service you provide you have to give the name of the service, estimated duration, price etc. When consumers search for you in jaldee the services created by you will be listed under your profile.

Q9) HOW CAN I DELETE A SERVICE?
A9) You cannot delete a service. Instead you can disable it. The disabled service will not be listed in your public profile.

Q10) WHAT DO MEAN BY QUEUES?
A10) Queue simply means the working hours of your enterprise.Services are rendered to the consumer in the order they checked-in online, walk-in consumers and phone-in consumers who checked-in at your desk.

Q11) CAN I ADD MORE THAN 1 QUEUE?
A11) You can more than 1 Queue and adjust your schedule accordingly through Wait list Manager Add New Queue Button.

Q12) HOW TO SET THE NUMBER OF CONSUMERS SERVED AT THE SAME TIME?
A12) While creating or editing a Queue you can set the number of consumers that can be served at a time and the waiting time will automatically get adjusted.

Q13) HOW TO LIMIT MAXIMUM NUMBER OF CONSUMERS TO BE SERVED FOR A DAY?
A13) While creating or editing a Queue you can set the maximum number of consumers to be served for a day. For example if we set 10 as the maximum capacity, only 10 consumers will be served for that Queue.

Q1) CAN I GIVE DISCOUNTS TO MY CUSTOMERS?
A1) Yes you can give discount to your customers, create discounts in Settings>Bill discounts which can be applied on customer bill.

Q2) CAN I SET TAX FOR MY SERVICES?
A2) Yes, you can set Tax for your services by enabling the tax applicable toggle button while creating the service. You can also set the tax to be collected in Tax Settings.

Q3) WHERE CAN ADD MY TAX SETTINGS?
A3) You can your GST number and tax percentage under Settings>Payment settings>Tax settings. These settings will be reflected on consumer bill.

For CC/DC/Net Banking fill out all the fields displayed there and save.
